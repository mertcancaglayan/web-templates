Travel FAQs

Answers to common travel questions

What's included in the tour package?

Our packages include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, and most meals. Flights and personal expenses are not included unless specified.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel up to 30 days before departure for a full refund. Cancellations within 14-29 days receive 50% refund. No refunds within 14 days of travel.

Do I need travel insurance?

Yes, comprehensive travel insurance is mandatory for all our tours. We can help you arrange coverage if needed.
